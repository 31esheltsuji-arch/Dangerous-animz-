Safety in the heart of Forest City

NO WI-FI REQUIRED

The Problem

In a forest city, dangerous animals like snakes and macaques can be anywhere. Traditional signs don't move, but animals do. We need a way to know what's around us in real-time to stay safe.

Our Solution

An app that uses GPS and an offline database to detect dangerous wildlife near your location. It identifies the animal and gives you instant safety instructions, even when you're deep in the woods without a signal.

Learn How It Works

Features

  • Animal Identification: Know if it's venomous instantly.
  • Proximity Alerts: Get a buzz when a dangerous animal was recently spotted nearby.
  • Survival Guide: Step-by-step instructions on what to do if you encounter a predator.

How To Use It

Simple Steps for Safety:

  • Open the App: The app pre-loads maps, so you don't need Wi-Fi.
  • Press "Check": The app scans for signals from animal trackers or community reports.
  • Read the Danger Level: If an animal is found, the app tells you if it is venomous or dangerous.
  • Follow Advice: Do exactly what the "Safety Tip" says to stay safe.
